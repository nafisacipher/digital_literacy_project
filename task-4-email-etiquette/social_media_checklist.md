
## Social Media Checklist for College Students

The following list presents recommended actions to students who use social media

	 •	Students should think about their social media posts because these online activities will shape their professional image.                
   
	•	Users should set their content visibility to maximum protection through strong privacy settings.
  
	•	Users must check information accuracy because sharing false information leads to spreading misinformation.	Users should show respect through their professional demeanor while communicating through comments and messages.
	Students should create a positive online presence through their digital achievements which they should share with others.

The following list presents actions that students should avoid according to social media guidelines

	•	Students should not reveal their personal information which includes their home address and phone number and identification documents.
  
	•	Users should avoid all forms of online bullying which includes both cyberbullying and virtual disputes.
  
	•	Users should not accept friend requests from unknown people without verification.
	Users should not publish any content that contains offensive material because it will damage their professional reputation.
	Users should avoid clicking on suspicious links or giveaway offers because these links and offers are probably scams.
